hike app

This app is used to record the activities of a hiking group. The app offers the possibility of registering users, writing posts, deleting, editing, commenting and liking. All activities are recorded on a map with the exact coordinates and other basic information. The frontend is written in Angular and is uploaded on Firebase. The backend is in Express.js and MongoDB and is uploaded on Heroku. It uses the Leaflet library for maps and images that are uploaded on Cludinary. Code
